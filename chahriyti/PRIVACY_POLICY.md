## 1. Overview

شهريتي (Chahriyti) is a personal salary management app that helps users track their monthly budget, expenses, debts, lendings, and savings goals. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

---

## 2. Data We Collect

### 2.1 Data You Enter
All financial data you enter into the app is stored **exclusively on your device**:

- Monthly salary and income
- Expenses and categories
- Debts and payment history
- Lendings and collection history
- Savings goals and balance
- Financial cycle history

**This data never leaves your device.** We do not collect, transmit, or store it on any server.

### 2.2 Device Identifier
The app reads a unique device identifier solely for **license key validation**. This validation is performed **entirely offline** on your device using a cryptographic algorithm (HMAC-SHA256). The device ID is never transmitted to any server.

### 2.3 Data We Do NOT Collect
- No personal information (name, email, phone)
- No location data
- No camera or microphone access
- No contacts access
- No usage analytics or crash reporting
- No advertising identifiers

---

## 3. How Data Is Used

| Data | Purpose | Shared with third parties? |
|------|---------|---------------------------|
| Financial data (expenses, debts, etc.) | Display budgets and statistics inside the app | ❌ Never |
| Device identifier | Offline license key validation | ❌ Never |

---

## 4. Data Storage

All app data is stored locally in a SQLite database on your device. Data is private to the app and is not accessible to other apps. Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all stored data.

---

## 5. Internet Access

The app requests no internet permissions. All features — including license validation — work entirely offline.

---

## 6. License Activation

The app uses a license key system. The license key is validated locally on your device using your device's unique identifier and a cryptographic algorithm. No data is sent to external servers during this process.

---

## 7. Local Notifications

The app may send local notifications to remind you of budget limits or upcoming financial events. These notifications are generated on your device and do not involve any external service.

---

## 8. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

---

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.

## 2. البيانات التي نتعامل معها

### 2.1 البيانات التي تدخلها
جميع بياناتك المالية تُخزَّن **على جهازك فقط**:

- الراتب الشهري والدخل الإضافي
